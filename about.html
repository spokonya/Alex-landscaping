Alex Blagrove

Owner

I’m Alex Blagrove, and since 2016 I’ve been helping families and businesses transform their outdoor spaces into places they’re proud to call their own. What started with a simple passion for hard work and quality has grown into a trusted landscaping and hardscaping company serving Brighton, NY and the surrounding communities.

With nearly a decade of hands-on experience, I take pride in delivering results that balance beauty, function, and long-term value. From complete landscape makeovers to detailed maintenance, my mission is always the same: to create outdoor spaces that enhance everyday living.

As a husband, father, and man of faith, I run my business with integrity, transparency, and a commitment to excellence. My goal is not only to improve properties, but also to build lasting relationships with the families and homeowners I serve.

At Alex Blagrove Landscaping & Lawn Service, we believe your outdoor space should be more than just a yard—it should be a reflection of your lifestyle, a gathering place for family and friends, and an investment you can be proud of.
